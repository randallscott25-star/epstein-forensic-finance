Data Sources & Methodology

Wire transfer data: Master Wire Ledger Phase 5I — 481 entity-resolved wires from Deutsche Bank SAR Exhibits A–E (EFTA00027019, DB-SDNY production). Entity resolution mapped 228 unique counterparties across 14 banking institutions.

Balance sheet computation: External In = sum of wires from entities classified as EXTERNAL_PARTY → shell entities. External Out = sum of wires from shell entities → EXTERNAL_PARTY. Shell In/Out = inter-shell transfers. Net = (External In + Shell In) − (External Out + Shell Out).

Publication Ledger context: The 481-wire master ledger ($558M) is one component of the broader publication ledger (6,310 unique transactions, $2.146B across 10 payment types). This visualization focuses exclusively on the wire transfer subset from the Deutsche Bank production.

Limitations: This balance sheet reflects only wire transfers visible in the Deutsche Bank SDNY production. Other banks' wire activity, non-wire transactions (checks, CHIPS, SWIFT), and sealed/destroyed records are not included. The $209M gap is an observation about what is documented, not a claim about what occurred.
